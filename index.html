Financial Intelligence Maturity

Where Is Bolton Clarke Today —
And Where Should It Be?

Large, transaction-intensive finance functions typically sit at Stage 2 — controlled and stable, but still heavily manual. The opportunity is to move to Stage 3: real-time intelligence embedded across existing workflows, without disrupting the systems already in place.

1
Stage 1 · Reactive
Manual & Retrospective
Finance operates on historical data. Month-end close is labour-intensive. Exceptions and anomalies are caught late — after cost or risk has already materialised.
  • Spreadsheet-driven reconciliations
  • Manual payroll validation cycles
  • No real-time AP or GL oversight
  • Reporting assembled from scratch
Common starting point
2
Stage 2 · Controlled
Standardised & Governed
Core systems are in place. ERP, payroll, Microsoft tools are operational. Controls exist — but remain largely manual. Compliance is maintained at high effort cost.
  • ERP, payroll & AP platforms live
  • Defined approval workflows
  • Consistent close process — but slow
  • Audit-ready with significant effort
← Bolton Clarke today
3
Stage 3 · Intelligent
Real-Time & Automated
An AI coordination layer sits above existing systems — monitoring in real time, automating routine governance, and surfacing exceptions before they become problems.
  • Continuous GL & payroll monitoring
  • Duplicate payment detection live
  • Automated close tracking & alerts
  • Executive dashboards updated in real time
→ Target: 6–12 months
4
Stage 4 · Strategic
Predictive & Forward-Looking
Finance drives business decisions. Predictive labour cost modelling, occupancy-linked forecasting, and entity-level benchmarking transform the finance function into a strategic asset.
  • Predictive labour & occupancy modelling
  • ANZ entity performance benchmarking
  • AI-assisted planning & scenario analysis
  • Automated multi-entity consolidation
→ Ambition: 12–24 months
Why This Approach, Why Now
Reduce manual overhead without replacing core systems
Accelerate close cycles and free finance capacity for strategic analysis
Strengthen payroll governance across 16,000 employees at scale
Improve cost visibility and margin control across ANZ entities
Build AI capability incrementally — disciplined, controlled, and measurable
For an organisation of Bolton Clarke's scale, small efficiency gains per transaction compound quickly. The opportunity is not transformation — it is precision optimisation of what already exists.
